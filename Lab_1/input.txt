Yaroslav the Wise street is a street in the Railway district of Lviv. It begins with a T-shaped intersection with Horodotska Street, then several small streets and squares join it - Golubovycha, Morozenka, Knyaz Svyatoslav Square, Gushalevych, Magazinova. It ends at a four-way intersection with Shevchenko and Yaroslav Pstrak streets.
